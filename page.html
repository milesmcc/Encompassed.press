Encompassed.press

The single most encompassing and important news story, found automatically.

Idea

Often in the news there is one single story that is significantly more "relevant" than the others, and Encompassed.press gives this one story to you. Instead of being bombarded by a page of headlines, Encompassed.press gives you just one article, the article it considers to be the most encompassing of the news -- the media condensed into one story.

Process

Which story is the most encompassing or relevant is subjective, so there is no such thing as "the MOST important story." Encompassed.press uses artificial intelligence in order to rank stories and then find the most important one (from its perspective, of course).

The Algorithm

To find the most important story, Encompassed.press scans lots of media outlets for their headlines. From this data, it tokenizes and stems the headlines (running, runs, and run all become run, for example) and then based on the frequency of the terms within each headline within the larger media corpus of all headlines, a "relevance score" is found. This score is then weighted against the "reputability" of the news source (which, I know, is completely subjective). Then, the article with the highest score is displayed.

Frequency

To avoid sending too many requests to the media outlets, the algorithm is run and the top story is refreshed every 30 minutes.
